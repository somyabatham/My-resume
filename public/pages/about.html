About Me

My academic background also includes strong performances in Class XII and Class X from C.B.S. Convent H.S. School, Gwalior.


During my studies, I have honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, and various other front-end technologies. I have a keen eye for design and a commitment to delivering exceptional user experiences. My hands-on experience includes an internship at Intelligentbots, where I worked as a Stream Trainee in robotics engineering, programming, and application development. This role allowed me to collaborate with experienced professionals and gain practical knowledge in cutting-edge technologies.


My project portfolio showcases my versatility in web development, including projects like "Gwalior Basket," an e-commerce platform for groceries, "E-LEARNING," an online education platform, and various other interactive web applications such as a birthday card creator, a number guessing game, and a background color changer component in React.


I am self-motivated, adaptable, and always eager to learn new things. My achievements include certifications in C Language from Great Learning, an internship at Intelligentbots, a course on Cyber Shikhaa from Quick Heal Academy, and training in Supply Chain Management from Shriram Group of College. I actively participate in co-curricular events, demonstrating my commitment to personal and professional growth.


With my proficiency in front-end technologies, I blend creativity with functionality, ensuring that each project resonates with its target audience. I am looking forward to opportunities where I can collaborate with creative teams and bring digital visions to life.
